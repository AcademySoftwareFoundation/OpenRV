# UTV Project Roles and Responsibilities

UTV is a fork of OpenRV which is a project of the Academy Software Foundation but is not beholden to the Open Review Initiative or the ASWF.

## Contributors

UTV grows and thrives from assistance from Contributors. Contributors include anyone in the community who contributes code, documentation, or other technical artifacts that have been incorporated into the project's repository.  Anyone can be a Contributor.

We highly encourage contributions that are also submitted as PR's to OpenRV.

### How to Become a Contributor

* Open an issue to discuss your idea or comment on existing issues
* Submit your code as a PR
